Urban Life in New Orleans tells the raw, unfiltered story of growing up in a city where poverty, drugs, and violence are everyday struggles. Dip through the wicked streets of New Orleans, where three friends are embedded into poverty. Having only the streets for guidance they stand together through the toughest times but their loyalty comes to a halt especially once the money gets low and the bricks stop running. So intense you didn't have to touch them to feel their pain. Yet, in the streets the one's to lift you up are usually the one's to gun you down.

Written by Gnoski, this story is both a cautionary tale and an inspiration — encouraging young people to make choices that lead away from crime and toward a brighter future.
